Scientist, Cancer Immunotherapy

We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced scientist to join QLSF Biotherapeutics located in South San Francisco. The preferred candidate will have deep knowledge in immunology or cancer research with extensive hands-on experience on target validation and drug development.

Responsibilities

  • Identify and adopt in vitro and in vivo biological assays to facilitate target discovery and elucidate the mechanism(s) of action.
  • Work independently on the design, execution, and interpretation of experimental results.
  • Stay abreast of latest scientific and technology advancements in the field.
  • Attend team meetings and deliver clear and well prepared presentations. Be an active participant in discussions.
  • Experience with ELISA assays. Experience of affinity, epitope binning analysis by Octet is a plus.

Qualifications

  • Position/Title commensurate with education and experience
  • Extensive hands-on experience in basic immunology techniques including flow cytometry, cytokine analysis, cytotoxicity and proliferation assays, etc.
  • Previous experience in antibody drug discovery is a plus.
  • Strong motivation with a dedicated work ethic.
  • Excellent oral presentation and communication skills

Physical Requirements:

  • Continuous sitting or standing for prolonged periods (more than 2 consecutive hours in an 8 hour day)
  • Travel: < 10% of the time
